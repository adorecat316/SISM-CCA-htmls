Music Band

Harmony in Every Heart

1) Music Band CCA is an activity where students would form a group of different instruments and play songs of various genres. In Music Band CCA, students will be able to experiment on and improvise music together, making exciting covers of pop songs while having fun coming up with new ideas. Also, students will learn about each others’ favorite styles and artists, thus introducing their band mates to new and interesting songs, singers, bands and styles.

2) The Music Band CCA will be on Mondays from 4.30-5.30pm. It would be held in the Music Practice Room, and the teacher adviser is Teacher Zhi Hong.

3) The Music Band CCA is suitable for students with musical experience in pop instruments (piano, guitar, drums, bass, vocals etc) and students with the desire to pick up any of these instruments as a hobby, interest or passion. Students who join would have fun while working alongside band mates and friends who share the same joy and passion playing pop songs of both classic and modern styles. The Music Band CCA creates an exciting energy and atmosphere that when the music starts, everyone would be enjoying themselves to the groove of the music.

4) Being a part of Music Band CCA would nurture students to work as a team, not just while playing music but also when discussing new ideas and plans. Besides that, students would also develop discipline and dedication as the Music Band CCA focuses on preparing for events and performances that would demand both qualities, as well as consistent commitment and attendance. Furthermore, students would learn to be more open-minded and creative as they work their way with music that can be altered and changed to suit their band’s instrumental setup. Finally, students would gain knowledge of setting up different combinations of instruments for a performance depending on various factors including band size, venue size, music styles and so on.
