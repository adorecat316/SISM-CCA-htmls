Choir

Sing in harmony and participate in concerts.

Definition, Description and Objective

Definition:

  • The Soka Melody Makers is a mixed youth choir, consisting of male and female students singing together in harmony across different voice parts (soprano, alto, tenor, bass).

Description:

  • Students learn to sing in a group while developing proper vocal technique, harmony, and stage presence. The choir performs a variety of music, including pop, contemporary, cultural, and choral pieces, and takes part in school events, concerts, competitions, and community programmes

Objectives:

  • Develop students’ vocal and musical abilities
  • Build teamwork, discipline, responsibility, and commitment
  • Enhance confidence and performance skills
  • Provide exposure to different music styles and cultural experiences
  • Support emotional wellbeing and self-expression

Activity Day, Time, Venue, Teacher Advisor

  • Activity Day: Tuesday
  • Time: 4.30 pm – 5.30 pm
  • Venue: C207
  • Teacher Advisor: Tr Jacqueline Teng

Suitable for Which Students & Why

Suitable for:

  • Students who enjoy singing, even if they have no prior training
  • Students who like teamwork and collaborative activities
  • Students who want to build confidence and stage presence
  • Students who are curious about music but don’t play instruments
  • Students who want to grow into disciplined, responsible, and respectful individuals

Membership & Participation Criteria

  • Slice of Art is open to students with an interest in art and creative exploration, regardless of prior experience
  • Students are expected to participate responsibly, follow instructions, and respect shared materials and spaces
  • For activities such as off-campus sketching, parental consent may be required
  • Teachers reserve the right to assess a student’s suitability for certain activities based on safety, behaviour, and readiness

Why join:

  • Beginner-friendly; no prior experience needed
  • Provides opportunities to perform and represent the school
  • Builds discipline, responsibility, and commitment through regular practice
  • Supports emotional wellbeing and encourages healthy self-expression
  • Develops focus, social skills, empathy, and confidence

Skills to be Gained:

Musical Skills:

  • Vocal technique (breathing, tone, control)
  • Harmony and ensemble singing
  • Rhythm and pitch accuracy
  • Musicianship skills

Personal & Character Development:

  • Discipline, responsibility, and commitment
  • Confidence and stage presence
  • Emotional awareness and self-expression
  • Respect for others and teamwork

Social & Leadership Skills:

  • Teamwork and cooperation
  • Leadership opportunities (section leaders, student mentors)
  • Communication and collaboration

Singing in The Soka Melody Makers is about more than just music - it’s about making friends, sharing moments, and creating harmony together. Every rehearsal brings laughter, energy, and the thrill of turning voices into something beautiful. Here, music is meaningful, hearts are connected, and the fun comes naturally with every note!
