Archery

Aim True, Shoot Strong, Hit Success!

Archery Club Overview

  • Archery at Soka International School provides students with the opportunity to learn and practice the sport of archery in a safe, structured, and supportive environment. Archery is a precision sport that focuses on proper technique, concentration, and self-discipline. Under guided instruction, students will learn the fundamentals of shooting, equipment handling, safety rules, and sportsmanship.

Benefits for Students

Participating in the Archery Club offers many physical and mental benefits, including:

  • Improved focus, concentration, and patience
  • Better hand-eye coordination and posture
  • Development of self-control, discipline, and confidence
  • Stress relief and mental calm through controlled practice
  • Encouragement of responsibility and respect for rules and safety

Goals of club

Archery club aims to:

  • Introduce students to the basics of archery in a safe and enjoyable way
  • Build strong fundamentals in technique and safety awareness
  • Encourage personal growth, confidence, and perseverance
  • Promote healthy extracurricular involvement and lifelong interest in sports
  • Foster teamwork, respect, and positive values among students

Activity schedule

  • Day: Every Thursday
  • Time: 4.30 pm – 5.30 pm

Archery welcomes students of all skill levels and is designed to support both beginners and those who wish to further develop their archery skills.
