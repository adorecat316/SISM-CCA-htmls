Cultural Club

One World, Many Stories.

Definition, Description & Objectives

Definition & Description:

  • The Cultural Club is a CCA that provides students with opportunities to explore, appreciate, and celebrate diverse cultures through hands-on activities, creative projects, traditions, and intercultural communication. The club encourages respect for diversity while helping students understand both their own culture and those of others.

Objectives:

  • To promote cultural awareness, respect, and open-mindedness
  • To promote cultural awareness, respect, and open-mindedness
  • To build a sense of belonging and global citizenship
  • To develop communication, collaboration, and leadership skills
  • To develop communication, collaboration, and leadership skills

Suitable Students & Reasons to Join

Suitable for students who:

  • Are curious about different cultures and traditions
  • Enjoy creative activities such as crafts, language, music, food, or celebrations
  • Prefer a non-competitive and inclusive club environment
  • Want to build confidence in communication and teamwork
  • Enjoy learning through hands-on experiences rather than exams

Why students should join:

  • To broaden their global perspective and cultural understanding
  • To make new friends across different backgrounds
  • To express creativity in a relaxed and supportive setting
  • To gain meaningful experiences beyond the classroom
  • To prepare for a multicultural world with empathy and respect

Skills to be gained

Students participating in the Cultural Club will develop:

  • Cultural awareness and global understanding
  • Communication and presentation skills
  • Creativity and problem-solving skills
  • Teamwork and collaboration
  • Confidence and leadership skills
  • Respect, empathy, and social responsibility

Examples of Activities:

  • Cultural crafts and decoration projects
  • Celebrations of cultural festivals (e.g. Lunar New Year, international events)
  • Language and cultural exchange activities
  • Collaborative art and display projects
