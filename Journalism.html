Journalism Club

Your Story, Our Mission

Journalism Club Vision

  • The Journalism Club envisions a dynamic and inclusive platform where students develop a strong voice and a deep appreciation for truthful, responsible reporting. Our vision is to nurture passionate young journalists who are committed to accuracy, creativity, and ethical storytelling. We aim to empower members with the skills and confidence to explore meaningful issues, highlight diverse perspectives, and produce high‑quality content that reflects the pulse of the school community.

Learning Objectives

Through participation in Journalism, students will:

  • Develop Strong Writing and Communication Skills
  • Strengthen Critical Thinking and Research Skills
  • Promote Collaboration and Leadership
  • Expand Awareness of School and Community Issues
  • Prepare Students for Real‑World Communication Challenges

Skills to be gained

  • Writing & Communication Skills
  • Research & Investigation Skills
  • Critical Thinking & Analysis
  • Photography & Multimedia Skills

Suitable Students & Participation

  • The Journalism Club is ideal for students who have a strong interest in writing and enjoy expressing their thoughts through various forms of storytelling. It welcomes individuals who are curious about current events, whether within the school community or the wider world, and who are eager to explore and discuss issues that matter. Students who enjoy conducting research, asking questions, and discovering new information will find the club especially engaging. Participation also suits those who are willing to develop new skills such as interviewing, photography, design, and digital media. The club encourages teamwork, so students who work well with others, share ideas, and give constructive feedback will thrive in this environment. Commitment and responsibility are also important, as members are expected to meet deadlines, follow ethical guidelines, and contribute actively to meetings and publication activities.

  • Venue: D 103
  • Teacher Advisor: Leorita Pauline A/P Anthony

Future Opportunities

  • Opportunities to pursue careers in journalism, media, communications, and publishing.
  • Development of strong writing and research skills useful for academic success.
  • Enhanced confidence and public‑speaking abilities that support future leadership roles.
