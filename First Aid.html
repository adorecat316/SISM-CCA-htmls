First Aid Club

Learn to Help, Be a Hero.

Definition, Description & Objectives

  • First Aid Club is an activity that teaches students basic first aid and Basic Life Support (BLS).
  • The objective is to help students learn how to respond calmly and correctly during emergencies and to increase awareness of health and safety.

Suitable Students & Why Join

Suitable for students who:

  • Suitable for students who are interested in learning life-saving skills. Students should join to gain useful knowledge, confidence, and the ability to help others during emergencies.

Skills to be gained

  • Basic Life Support (BLS)
  • Basic first aid skills
