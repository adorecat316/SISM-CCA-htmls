Sports

Badminton

Join our badminton team and compete in inter-school tournaments.

Badminton Club Vision

  • To develop discipline, confidence and a strong positive character through badminton
  • Encouraging more on teamwork, sportsmanship and respect for one another
  • Building and learning new badminton skills from all levels
  • To build a strong and healthy body
  • Share more of a Active Lifestyle through sports

Learning objectives

  • Learning to develop a proper badminton technique and to be able to execute the move correctly
  • Proper Footwork understanding to be able to move in court properly and efficiently
  • Learning basic game tactical and strategies for matches that are more on advanced level
  • Improving on eyes coordination, speed, agility and endurance
  • Building confidence, focus, mental strength and resilience for training and matches

Skills to be gained

1. Badminton skills

  • Correct grip, footwork, and body positioning
  • Basic & Fundamental strokes (clear, drop, smash, net shots, defense)
  • Improved shot accuracy, timing, and consistency

2. Physical skills

  • Better stamina and overall fitness
  • Speed, agility, balance, and coordination
  • Strength and flexibility for safe movement

3. Mental and Social skills

  • Game awareness and better and stronger decision-making
  • Focus, confidence, and resilience
  • Teamwork, communication, and respect among each other

Which and why should students join?

1. Which students should join?

  • Students of all skill levels, from beginners to competitive players
  • Students that are interested in learning badminton in a structured way
  • Students who enjoy staying active and healthy
  • Students that are preparing for school or inter-school competitions

2. Why students should join?

  • Learning badminton through safe, structured, and age-appropriate coaching
  • Improving fitness, coordination, and confidence
  • Developing discipline, teamwork, and sportsmanship
  • Build focus, resilience, and a positive mindset
  • Enjoy a fun and supportive environment while developing skills
