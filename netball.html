Sports

Netball

Step onto the Court, Step into the Action.

Definition,Description,Objectives

1. Definition

  • Netball CCA is a sports where students learn and play netball in an organized and structured environment. It focuses on developing skills, teamwork, fitness, discipline and sportsmanship through training sessions, drills and games.

2. Description

  • Netball CCA aims to develop students’ interest and ability in netball through regular training sessions, skill-based drills, and game play. Students will learn the fundamental techniques of netball, understand game rules and positions, and participate in team activities that promote cooperation and discipline.

3. Objectives

  • To develop students’ basic and advanced netball skills
  • To improve physical fitness, agility, coordination and stamina
  • To build teamwork, communication and cooperation
  • To instill discipline, responsibility and sportsmanship
  • To enhance students’ understanding of netball rules, positions and strategies

Activity schedule

  • Day: Every Wednesday
  • Time: 3.30 pm – 4.30 pm
  • Venue: MPH (Harmony Hall)
  • Teacher advisor: Teacher Nisa and Teacher Mira

Suitable students and why they should join:

1. Suitable for students who:

  • Are interested in sports and physical activities
  • Want to learn a team-based game
  • Wish to improve their fitness and coordination
  • Want to build confidence and communication skills

2. Why students should join:

  • To stay active and healthy
  • To make new friends and develop team spirit
  • To learn important life values such as discipline, respect and responsibility
  • To improve focus, decision making and game strategies

Skills to be gained

1. Sports skills

  • Passing
  • Catching techniques
  • Shooting skills
  • Footwork & pivoting
  • Defending techniques
  • Positioning & movement on court

2. Physical skills

  • Speed & agility
  • Balance & coordination
  • Stamina & endurance
  • Reaction time

3. Personal & Social skills

  • Teamwork & cooperation
  • Communication skills
  • Leadership qualities
  • Discipline & responsibility
  • Confidence & resilience
