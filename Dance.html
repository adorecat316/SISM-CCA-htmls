Contemporary Dance

Every Move, A Story

Contemporary Dance Vision

  • To inspire students to explore movement, express themselves through dance, and develop confidence both on and off stage. Contemporary Dance provides a supportive environment where creativity, discipline, and collaboration come together, helping students build skills that extend beyond dance.

Learning Objectives / Goals

Through participation in Contemporary Dance, students will:

  • Build confidence and self-expression through movement
  • Learn and explore different dance techniques and styles
  • Improve body awareness, coordination, and flexibility
  • Develop creativity through improvisation and choreography
  • Strengthen teamwork and communication through group dances
  • Experience discipline, perseverance, and reflection through practice and performance

Skills to be Gained

  • Dance & Physical Skills: Technique, balance, coordination, flexibility, musicality, and expressive movement
  • Creative Skills: Improvisation, choreography, storytelling through movement, and creative problem-solving
  • Social & Personal Skills: Confidence, resilience, collaboration, communication, and respect for peers and rehearsal spaces

Suitable Students & Participation

  • Open to any student with an interest in dance, regardless of prior experience
  • Students should be willing to participate actively, respect rehearsal spaces, and work collaboratively
  • Ideal for students who enjoy music, movement, and creative expression and want to challenge themselves physically and artistically

Activity Details

  • Day & Time: Monday, 3.30pm - 4.30pm
  • Venue: Drama & Dance Studio
  • Teacher Advisor: Caren Yap Chai Wen

Sessions typically include warm-ups, technique exercises, choreography practice, creative exploration, and reflection

Future Opportunities

  • Participation in school performances, showcases, and competitions
  • Leadership roles for senior students (e.g., Dance Mentors or Assistant Choreographers)
  • Opportunities to collaborate with other CCAs like music or theatre

Why join:

  • Beginner-friendly; no prior experience needed
  • Provides opportunities to perform and represent the school
  • Builds discipline, responsibility, and commitment through regular practice
  • Supports emotional wellbeing and encourages healthy self-expression
  • Develops focus, social skills, empathy, and confidence
