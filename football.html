Sports

Football

Train with our coaches and represent the school in matches.

Football Club Vision

  • To build a founding love and knowledge for football as a sport.
  • To develop the basics of football.
  • To develop fair play, team spirit and camaraderie in a team.

Learning objectives

  • To learn and develop the 4 basics of football which are, first touch, ball mastery, striking the ball and 1 v 1 ability.
  • To develop the general camaraderie in a team, to maintain good team morale and better fostering of teamwork.

Skills to be gained

  • Communication Skills.
  • Cardiovascular improvement.
  • Technique with the ball.

Suitable students and participation

  • The ECA club is a good first step for novice and beginners from both genders (both and girl) to learn the basics in football. It is crucial that the students who are eager to learn football must take this opportunity to learn and practice the basics in football as it takes years to master the basics. For any football player who intends to push their limits, their technique with the ball is a critical stage of their development before moving to learning the gameplay and tactics implemented by the coaches.
